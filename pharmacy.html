Our Solutions



Many hospitals have strict budgets and patient flows, and as a result, they are unable to offer quality pharmaceutical services 24/7. Even in retail pharmacies, there is too much work and not enough time paying attention to YOUR needs. WorldMed Communciation’s Telepharmacy Integrated Software™ provides these health providers an opportunity to help you in your pharmaceutical needs. Our cloud-based data storage system allows prescription verification details, medication records and reviews, substitution requests, and an online scheduling service for flu shots and immunizations. Through this innovation, we are able to maximize the productivity of these healthcare providers and bring you quality medical attention.

Experienced and knowledgeable pharmacists worldwide have partnered with us through our health professional specialist network to provide you the best possible patient experience at WorldMed Communications. We offer a Call-Only service 24/7 to answer all of our concerns. We also offer this solution to local hospitals and retail pharmacies to bring these services to you locally.

WorldMed Communications aims to streamline and facilitate the pharmacist connection process through our Pharmacist Connect™ patient connection program. We also offer secure and private video conferencing capabilities for remote consultations and second opinions.
