Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1986, WorldMed Communications strives for equity in healthcare access and quality medical attention as well as patient experience. We are dedicated to serving everyone across the globe through our presence in the telemedicine industry. WorldMed Communications has partnered with several well-known organizations and companies to provide you the best possible experience, including, but not limited to, the CDC, WHO, Vonage, and the American Red Cross.

Our CEO's Journey

As a child, his parents sadly passed away due to an inability to provide them quality healthcare immediately. From then on, our founder was dedicated to ensuring this never happened to anyone else. For days and weeks on end, he worked tirelessly to design WorldMed Communications first telemedicine software in 1995. Multiple organizations were impressed in his work and invested his idea.

Partnerships

Our CEO collaborated with over 20 software developers and health professionals to develop WorldMed Communication’s first iteration of the Health Professional Speciality Network™ and launched the main website platform in 2003. Over 10,000 users registered on the first day, proving his dedicated work. From then, our founder and current President built a wide network of health professionals and employees to expand on our current services.
