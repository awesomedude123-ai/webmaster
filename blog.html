In Progress! WorldMed Communication Blog

This past month, WorldMed Communications is currently working on releasing a blog within our website platform to raise awareness about telemedicine, virtual healthcare, and best practices to increase health and well-being. Specifically, we will be focusing on raising awareness on the CDC and the WHO policies concerning the recent COVID19 outbreak. Our blog will be run by the top bloggers and reporters from across the world to deliver quality content to you. We will be uploading three times DAILY to ensure that you are informed and cared for.

We will also be linking our blog to our social media accounts on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter to broaden our reach and impact even further. Wherever you are, you can reap all of the benefits and services we offer here at WorldMed Communications. Take advantage of these opportunities!

We will also be spreading promotional flyers and webinars through our website as we are currently working on enabling a video conferencing and webinar capability within our website platform. Users from across the globe can meet together and learn more about what services we offer at WorldMed Communications, as well as general information on well-being, health, and telemedicine. This functionality will also be available on the WorldMed Communications mobile application. Simply download the app from the App Store and Google Play Store today!
