American Red Cross

In August of 2020, WorldMed Communications partnered with the American Red Cross to raise awareness about their efforts and develop an effective and streamlined platform where donors can register for blood drives.

For the past half-year, American Red Cross has been looking to host blood drives in order to test for possible antibodies to provide new opportunities for patients battling COVID-19. We here at WorldMed Communications acknowledge their efforts and are eager to work with them to extend their reach and serve all.

In September of 2020, WorldMed Communications officially launched its American Red Cross Donor Network™ service on the WorldMed Communications mobile application. This resource allows donors on our platform to immediately register for blood drives in their area with a click of a button. Our cloud-based data storage and medical information design allows easy registration without filling out any details. Our platform immediately sends all pertinent medical information to the American Red Cross, who then processes them and coordinates with you, the donor, for next steps on the donation process.

Our collaboration with the American Red Cross has been extremely successful. The organization has reported over a 25.7% increase in donations per day, along with a similar percent increase in liters of blood donated daily. Now, the American Red Cross has been able to serve other patients who need blood and test for possible antibodies in the fight against COVID-19.

President of the American Red Cross, Gail J. McGovern, said the following about the impact of WorldMed Communications on their efforts:

“Thanks to WorldMed Communications, we’ve seen tremendous increases in donations and blood. We’re now able to serve so many people and help in the fight against COVID-19. Organizations like WorldMed Communications are the reason why the healthcare industry is fighting now more than ever in the effort to provide equity in healthcare services. The American Red Cross thanks WorldMed Communications for all their help.”

WorldMed Communications sincerely appreciates these comments from Mrs. McGovern, and we will continue to work hard in collaboration with the American Red Cross.

Download the WorldMed Communications mobile application on the App Store and Google Play Store today! Go to the American Red Cross Donor Network™ channel on the app and schedule your donation today!
