Health Systems

At WorldMed Communications, we are dedicated to providing you the best possible healthcare experience through our innovative technologies and remote solutions. From accessing all of our services through your mobile phone to partnering with local health providers to offer virtual care solutions, we strive to revolutionize the telemedicine industry. Learn more about these innovations and leverage them to maximize your experience!

WorldMed Communications App

At WorldMed Communications, we believe in equity for all of our patients in terms of quality healthcare experiences and medical attention. To that end, we’ve partnered with Vonage™ telecommunications to develop the WorldMed Communications App™. Equipped with Voice, Video, and Messages APIs, our application is able to provide the best possible patient experience for you! Leverage the app to call or video conference health professionals, receive notifications through SMS, WhatsApp, or Facebook Messenger on your status, and store medical information and pay within our PCI and HIPAA compliant cloud-based platform. Download on IOS and Android today!

Remote Patient Management Solutions

WorldMed Communications aims to offer solutions to all of our patients, especially those with chronic conditions that necessitate immediate and consistent attention. Our Remote Monitoring Care Service provides patient engagement and early treatment interventions by offering data analytics to health providers and patients, thereby empowering them to initiate the appropriate stages of treatment

Virtual Care Solutions for Health Providers

With WorldMed Communications, local health providers can offer a streamlined and quality health experience to all patients around the globe. We have partnered with over 1,500 hospitals and care centers and have provided them special access to our medical record and data tracking software, called the WorldMed Communications Virtual Care Software™. We are working hard to empower local centers with the tools necessary to ensure safe and effective healthcare.
