COVID-19 Treatment Initiative

With COVID-19 affecting millions of people worldwide, WorldMed Communications is dedicated to providing quality healthcare at a time when patients need it the most. We’ve partnered with thousands of local health providers worldwide and expanded our distribution network to deliver COVID-19 test kits to help with testing for COVID-19 in patients.

We’ve also added a new COVID-19 news and treatment channel within our WorldMed Communications mobile application that will allow patients to schedule virtual check-ups and receive consultations. At WorldMed Communications, we’re doing everything we can to support those affected during the pandemic.

Telemedicine is especially important for addressing this COVID-19 pandemic because it minimizes contact with health professionals, facilities, and other patients. That way, we can ensure quality healthcare and patient experience right from your house.

Our video conferencing and phone capabilities on our mobile application allows you to contact health professionals through video or through a live chat. We can then remotely monitor your status and progress on treatment if necessary. All of our services are monitored and approved by the CDC and WHO.

What kind of services does WorldMed Communications offer specifically for the COVID-19 outbreak? Here is a brief list for your reference:

  • COVID-19 screening and testing
  • Testing recommendations and guidance
  • Isolation and quarantine help
  • General health visits for symptoms of COVID-19
  • Medication prescriptions (in association with your local health provider)
  • Nutrition consultations
  • Mental health and stress consultations


Download the WorldMed Communications mobile application on the App Store and Google Play Store today! Use our resources and receive your free COVID-19 consultation and test!
